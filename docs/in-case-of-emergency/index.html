In Case of Emergency

Emergency Call-Out Number

Official Agencies requesting our assistance on a search, or looking for more information about our team, please dial: (918) 928-2998.

Family and friends of missing persons, please contact your local law enforcement agency and let them know you’d like our help.

Please DO NOT call the number above for any non-emergency needs, such as donations, sales, pet adoptions, or media inquiries. Those needs and questions can be directed to us via email or Facebook, where they’ll be routed to the appropriate team member. We do not accept pets as donations.

Confidentiality

Details of all search call outs are kept strictly confidential and discreetly handled within our team. You can count on us to respect the privacy of any search subject and their families, to the best of our abilities.
